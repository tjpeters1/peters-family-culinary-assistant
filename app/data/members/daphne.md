# Daphne's Culinary Profile (Kid)

* Plain Cheese Quesadillas (melted cheese in flour tortilla)
* Chicken Nuggets
* Hot Dogs (plain beef hot dog in bun)

* Complex sauces or heavily seasoned dishes with visible green herbs

* **Taco Loco** (Plain Cheese Quesadilla)
* **Burger Town** (French Fries and Chocolate Milkshake)

## Preferred Quick Alternative Meal
