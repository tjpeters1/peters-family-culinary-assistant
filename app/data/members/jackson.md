# Jackson's Culinary Profile (Kid)

## Favorite Dishes & Guidelines
* Creamy Macaroni and Cheese
* Classic Cheese Pizza
* Beef Burgers (plain with cheese and ketchup)
* Beef Tacos (plain ground beef, cheese, flour tortilla)

## Dislikes & Dietary Restrictions
* Spicy food (any hot spices or chiles)
* Visible vegetables (onions, zucchini, peppers — will refuse to eat)
* Seafood (salmon, shrimp, or fish of any kind)

## Favorite Takeout Restaurants
* **Pizza Bella** (Always orders the classic Cheese Pizza)
* **Burger Town** (Excellent beef burgers, crispy French fries, and vanilla milkshakes)

## Preferred Quick Alternative Meal
* Boxed Mac and Cheese
