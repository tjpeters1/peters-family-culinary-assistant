# Nikki's Culinary Profile (Parent & Cook)

* Roasted Mediterranean Veggies (zucchini, bell peppers, tomatoes)
* Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

*Note: Use these favorite dishes as inspiration, but feel free to suggest other dishes that follow similar fresh, healthy flavor profiles!*
